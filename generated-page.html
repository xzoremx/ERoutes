Features

Built for freelancers, tuned for momentum

Smart, flexible, and built around your business workflow

Hide OnePro branding

Customize OnePro to feel like your studio. Adjust colors, toggle branding, and shape the interface so every client, project, and invoice runs through a system that looks and works the way your brand does.

Integrates seamlessly with the tools you already use

Plug OnePro into your existing stack in minutes. Connect Notion, Slack, Google, automation tools and more to sync data, trigger workflows, and keep your operations running without constant contextswitching.

Collaborate in realtime

Keep everyone on the same page with live comments, messages, and project threads. Clients, collaborators, and contractors see the same source of truth, so decisions move faster and nothing gets lost in DMs.

Speaks your language

Work in the currencies, time zones, and formats that match your world. OnePro adapts to your locale so invoices, dates, and reports feel nativewhether youre working locally or with clients across the globe.

View things your way

Toggle between Kanban, cards, lists, tables, timelines, or calendars to match how you think. Zoom out for a bigpicture pipeline or zoom in on the next taskyour data stays the same, only the perspective changes.
